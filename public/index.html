What Is WNH
📡
Intercept
Donated or recovered e-waste is received at a hub node, cataloged with a unique WNH asset ID, and logged into this inventory. Nothing goes undocumented.
🔧
Rescue
Hardware is assessed, triaged, and assigned to an engineer or project. Broken screens, dead batteries, bricked firmware — everything gets a shot at revival.
⚙️
Deploy
Rescued devices become compute nodes, testbeds for kernel experiments, embedded systems labs, or platform for low-level driver and OS research.
