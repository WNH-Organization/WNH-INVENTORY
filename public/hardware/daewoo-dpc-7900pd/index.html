The Daewoo portable DVD player is a self-contained multimedia device produced in the mid-2000s (around 2005–2007), intended for portable DVD and media playback with an integrated TFT display, speakers, and battery.

It has largely fallen out of use due to the rise of smartphones, streaming services, and solid-state media, which offer more convenient, compact, and flexible video playback. The TFT screen and optical drive motors are excellent salvage targets.
